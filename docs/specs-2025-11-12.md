# **Football Quiz Webapp — Consolidated Product Specifications (From This Second Conversation)**

## 1. **High-Level Vision**

A football quiz platform designed to:

* Let players instantly play quizzes on matches, events, clubs, etc.
* Let creators personalize and brand their quizzes.
* Make the experience viral through social sharing and QR codes.
* Monetise smartly via ads and creator-friendly revenue sharing.
* Support white-labeling and theming for influencers and content creators.

---

# 2. **Monetisation Strategy (Quiz Webapp)**

### 2.1 **No Bonus Mechanics**

* No tokens, no credits, no casino-like boosters.
* Pure quiz experience.

### 2.2 **Advertising**

* **Audio ads** during gameplay (high CPM, works even with low volume).
* Optional visual ads.
* Football content qualifies as **premium ad inventory**.

### 2.3 **Revenue for Creators**

* Creators can:

  * Share themed/white-label quizzes.
  * Earn a percentage of the ad revenue from quizzes they generate.
* Requires an account only when cashing out.

---

# 3. **Personalisation & White Label**

### 3.1 **Creator Personalisation**

Creators should be able to:

* Apply custom themes (colors, background, logo).
* White-label the quiz for their club, brand, or channel.
* Customize share cards, titles, intros, etc.

### 3.2 **User Theming (Future)**

* Allow users to submit their own visual themes.
* Potential for:

  * “Theme leaderboards”
  * Trending themes
* Inspired by MySpace / GeoCities style freedom.

### 3.3 **Impact on Virality**

* Custom themes increase:

  * Shareability
  * Creator pride
  * Social signaling (“This is MY quiz”)
* Aligns with the psychology of social media posting.

# 6. **Viral Sharing Requirements**

### 6.1 **QR Code Sharing**

* Creators must get a QR code for quick sharing:

  * TikTok Lives
  * Instagram Stories
  * Stadium screens
  * Twitch streams

### 6.2 **Link Sharing**

* Automatically generated shareable link with:

  * Preview image (OpenGraph)
  * Custom theme
  * Creator name/avatar
  * Match / club context

### 6.3 **After-Quiz Sharing**

Most important viral moment → **after completing a quiz**.
User gets:

* Score summary
* Comparison vs the quiz creator
* Leaderboard preview
* Giant “Share your score” button

# 7. **Advertising Integration (Smart & Minimal-Friction)**

### 7.1 **Audio Ads**

* Play short audio ads during answering.
* Works well with football content.

### 7.2 **Optional Display Ads**
